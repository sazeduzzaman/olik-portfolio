About & Me

I am SA Haque Alik — a filmmaker, screenwriter, and music composer with nearly two decades of experience in the Bangladeshi film and television industry. Since the early 2000s, I’ve worked to establish myself as a passionate and consistent storyteller. I began my journey in television and gradually transitioned to the big screen, where I’ve directed and written numerous acclaimed dramas, telefilms, and feature films that have connected deeply with audiences across the country.

My debut feature film, Hridoyer Kotha, was the first step in a series of heartfelt projects that combined emotional depth with strong, relatable storytelling. My goal has always been to craft narratives that not only entertain but also leave a lasting emotional impact. Whether I’m working on a romantic drama, a high-energy action film, or a moving family story, I focus on the human experience and emotional truth behind each frame.
Some of my most recognized works include Aro Bhalobashbo Tomay, Golui, Rongdhonu, and Ek Prithibi Prem. These films are not only known for their narratives but also for their music — many of which I composed myself. Beyond filmmaking, I take pride in mentoring emerging artists and contributing to the growth of our creative community. With over 18 years of active contribution to cinema and media, I continue to explore new ideas and push creative boundaries. My journey is driven by passion, resilience, and an enduring love for storytelling.
