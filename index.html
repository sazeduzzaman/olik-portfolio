S A Haque Olike

With over 18 years of experience in visual storytelling, I specialize in film direction, screenwriting, and composition.

My journey began in the late 1990s with television dramas, where I honed my skills in emotional narrative and visual design. Since then, I’ve directed several acclaimed films that reflect culture, heart, and human connection. I believe in blending creativity with purpose—every frame, line, and melody is crafted to resonate with the audience. My philosophy is simple: tell stories that move people and leave a lasting impact.

About My Work in Film & Music

I specialize in directing a wide range of films, including romantic dramas, thrillers, and character-driven narratives. I focus on stories that resonate emotionally with audiences, with a keen emphasis on powerful performances and visually captivating cinematography.
My approach to filmmaking is rooted in emotional depth and authenticity. I believe in creating stories that evoke real human emotions and connect deeply with the audience. I prioritize collaboration with my cast and crew to bring out the best in every scene and detail.
Balancing screenwriting and directing is a seamless process for me, as I’m deeply involved in both aspects of a film. Writing the script allows me to lay a strong foundation, while directing gives me the chance to bring that vision to life on screen. Both roles complement each other in crafting a compelling narrative.
Yes, I often compose original music for my films. Music plays a significant role in setting the emotional tone and enhancing the storytelling experience. I take great care in crafting scores that complement the visual storytelling of the film.
I believe that Bangladeshi cinema has immense potential for global recognition. My vision is to create films that showcase our unique culture while maintaining universal appeal. I aim to experiment with different genres and styles to help elevate the standard of local filmmaking and inspire the next generation of filmmakers.
